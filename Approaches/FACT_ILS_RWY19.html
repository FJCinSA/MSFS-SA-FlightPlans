MISSED APPROACH
Climb straight ahead on track 190° to 2000ft, then climbing RIGHT turn to 4000ft tracking CTV VOR. Hold at CTV VOR, 010° inbound, right turns. DO NOT TURN LEFT (west) — Table Mountain 3558ft. Notify Cape Town Approach.
⚠ PILOT CAUTIONS — CAPE TOWN
TERRAIN WEST:Table Mountain (3558ft) lies 6nm northwest. Prohibited from turning west below 4000ft during approach or missed approach.
TERRAIN EAST:Hottentots Holland range (peaks to 5700ft) approximately 30nm east. Stay on ILS centreline during approach.
RUNWAY:FACT has a secondary runway 16/34. Confirm RWY 19 in ATC clearance before commencing approach.
