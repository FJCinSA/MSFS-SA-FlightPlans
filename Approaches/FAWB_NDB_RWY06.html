MISSED APPROACH
Climb straight ahead on runway track 060° to 5500ft, then climbing right turn tracking WB NDB outbound on 240° radial to 6000ft. Hold at WB NDB, 060° inbound, left turns. Await further clearance from Wonderboom Approach.
⚠ PILOT CAUTIONS — WONDERBOOM
TAXIWAY:A taxiway runs parallel to RWY 06/24 and is similar in width and appearance. Pilots trained on single-strip aerodromes must positively identify runway markings (white numbers and centreline) before landing. Yellow markings = taxiway. Confirm with Tower if uncertain.
FIRST VISIT:Request progressive taxi instructions if unfamiliar with the aerodrome layout. Tower is approachable.
CIRCUIT:RWY 24 right-hand circuit. Confirm circuit direction in your clearance.
Historical note: Wonderboom is where Dr Francois Coetzee obtained his PPL. Originally a single-strip GA field, now upgraded to national airport status with extended runway capable of handling heavier traffic. The parallel taxiway that resembles a second runway has caught out more than one pilot transitioning from smaller strips — including a newly-licensed pilot who made a perfect landing on the taxiway on his first visit from Groblersdal. Aircraft and pilot undamaged. Lesson learned.
